# 👋 Welcome

<img src="{{ '/assets/img/profile.jpg' | relative_url }}" alt="Haripriyan Uthayakumar" width="200" style="border-radius: 50%; float: right; margin: 0 0 20px 20px;">

I am **Haripriyan Uthayakumar**, a Graduate Research Assistant at **Purdue University** in the **Department of Agricultural & Biological Engineering**.  
My research focuses on the intersection of **sustainability, stream ecology, machine learning, and circular economy in pharmaceuticals**.  

---

## 🔹 About Me
- 🎓 Pursuing **MS/PhD in Sustainable Industrial Natural Coupled Systems** at Purdue.  
- 🌊 Lead researcher for **NetConUS**, a connectivity-based stream classification system for the U.S.  
- 💊 Developer of **PharmaSort** and **EcoPharmaHub**, tools for sustainable pharmaceutical waste management.  
- 🤖 Passionate about **AI/ML for sustainability, industrial ecology, and circular supply chains**.  
- 🌍 Aim: to bridge **ecological conservation** with **sustainable chemical engineering**.
